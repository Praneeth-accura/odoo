Direct Loan payment

Here you can pay your loan installment(s) directly














1. Select your installment(s)

2. Click 'Proceed to pay'











3. Check the installment(s) at loan form, the status of installment(s) will be change to paid


Loan payment using Payslip(s)

Here installment(s) will be automatically paid while creating payslip(s)

1. Create payslip


1. Slip will be automatically select any pending loan installments between the range.


2. Click 'Confirm'




3. Check the installment(s) at loan form, the status of installment(s) will be change to paid
