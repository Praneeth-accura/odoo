POS Cashier and Salesperson

Cashier in POS

The login user or session responsible person will be considered as cashier. It is shown on top right corner.

Salesperson in POS

When we click on the sales person name, it will lists all pos users. So login user/ cashier can change the salesperson related to the sales. This helps to mark sales of related sales person with one login or one cashier.

Cashier in Order Form

In backend, it will show both sales person and cashier separately.

Cashier in Order list

In list, it is showing both sales person and cashier as separate columns.
